Privacy Policy

App: Intend  |  Last updated: March 2026  |  Contact: poshto711@gmail.com

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information Intend collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Intend stores all of the following exclusively on your device using your phone's local storage. None of this data is transmitted to any server or shared with any third party:

  • Your selected mood pack and daily intention word
  • Your intention history and streak count
  • Your personal journal reflections
  • Your notification time preference

You can permanently delete all of this data at any time via Settings → Reset all data, or by uninstalling the app.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Intend displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve relevant ads:

  • Android Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App interaction data (ad impressions and clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can review Google's data practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

To opt out of personalised advertising, go to your phone's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation. You can also reset your Advertising ID from the same menu.

3. No Account Required

Intend does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or log in. We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly.

4. No Third-Party Analytics

Intend does not use any analytics SDK (such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar). We do not track how you use the app beyond what AdMob collects for advertising purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

Intend is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

6. Data Security

All data stored locally is protected by your device's own security. Data transmitted between the AdMob SDK and Google's servers is encrypted in transit using TLS.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at poshto711@gmail.com.
